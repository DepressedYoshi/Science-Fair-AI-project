Conclusion

Graph one is a line graph representing the rounds of training and the accuracy of the Neural network to the testing dataset with different sizes of data set. This graph shows that the quality of the performance and quantity of the training data is proportional, and more training doesn't necessarily give the highest accuracy. If you examine table 2&3, you will see that the maximum percentage of accuracy is higher than the accuracy from final rounds of training, and it came from around twenty rounds of training. Which confirms the fact that larger training data is the key to better outcome rather than more training. On the aspect of classifying my doodling, the datatable (1) is chaotic. The percentage have a trend of going up with bigger dataset just like how neural network react to the testing dataset, but the numbers were significantly lower and fluctuate violently.the average accuracy was 61%, and again, we see that more training data definitely help increase the accuracy, but more training did not have a weighty influence on the outcome.One interesting remarks is that, I draw some chinese character for cat to use as a cat doodling during the experiment, and surprisingly, despite there are no chinese in the training data set and the character does not look like a cat, the algorithm was able to correctly classify it almost every time, and I repeated many times with different style and the result remain the same.
