What is
Neural Network?

Neural nets are a means of doing machine learning,
in which a computer learns to perform some task by
analyzing training examples. An object recognition system,
for instance, might be fed thousands of labeled images of
cars,houses, coffee cups, and it would find visual patterns in
the images that consistently correlate with particular labels.
